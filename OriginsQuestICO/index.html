Function of fragments for in game use: Once players start their quest to seek the Infinity Stones on the world wide web they will be handed their first debriefing package/information sheet to start. During the quest, players will be pointed to objective which will require clues or additional information to find the next objective, putting them closer to the ultimate goal, finding the Infinity Stones. Each of these clues and informational pieces will be strategically placed and or hidden all over the virtual universe(www) and once players find them, they will be required to sacrifice or pay an small amount of their fragments to obtain the information or clues to continue their objectives towards the quest. The fragments will also serve as the ultimate sacrificial currency that will be used to pay the guardian of the stone once a player finds the stone after completing all of his objectives.
